概述

厨房计划（"本应用"）是一款用于每周膳食规划、冰箱库存管理和购物清单生成的 iOS 应用。我们高度重视您的隐私，本隐私协议说明了本应用如何处理您的数据。

数据收集与存储

本应用不收集、不传输、不存储任何个人信息到远程服务器。您的所有数据（包括食谱、膳食计划、冰箱库存和购物清单）均存储在您的设备本地，使用 Apple 的 SwiftData 框架。

我们没有自己的服务器或后台服务，不会以任何方式访问您的数据。

Apple Intelligence

本应用使用 Apple Intelligence 提供 AI 食谱生成功能。该功能完全在您的设备上运行，由 Apple 的设备端模型处理，不会将您的输入内容发送到任何外部服务器。

第三方服务

本应用可能使用 Spoonacular API 获取食谱和食材数据。与该服务的通信仅限于获取食谱信息，不会发送您的任何个人数据。

  • Spoonacular 隐私政策：spoonacular.com/food-api/terms

数据删除

由于所有数据存储在您的设备本地，您可以随时通过删除应用来完全清除所有数据。

儿童隐私

本应用不针对 13 岁以下的儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

Overview

Proj. Kitchen ("the App") is an iOS application for weekly meal planning, fridge inventory management, and grocery list generation. We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection & Storage

The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on remote servers. All your data — including recipes, meal plans, fridge inventory, and shopping lists — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

We do not operate any servers or backend services and have no access to your data.

Apple Intelligence

The App uses Apple Intelligence to provide AI-powered recipe generation. This feature runs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device models. Your input is not sent to any external server.

Third-Party Services

The App may use the Spoonacular API to fetch recipe and ingredient data. Communication with this service is limited to retrieving recipe information and does not include any of your personal data.

  • Spoonacular Privacy Policy: spoonacular.com/food-api/terms

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can completely erase all data at any time by deleting the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
